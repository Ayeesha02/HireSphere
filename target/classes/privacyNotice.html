Privacy Notice

Welcome to our Hiring System. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data We Collect

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information (e.g., name, skills, experience for candidates; company details for recruiters)
  • Job applications and resumes (for candidates)
  • Audit logs of data processing activities

Purpose of Processing

We process your data to:

  • Facilitate job recruitment and matching
  • Provide AI-driven recommendations and screening
  • Manage user accounts and ensure system security
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., GDPR audits)

Lawful Basis

Data processing is based on your explicit consent, provided during registration, and our legitimate interest in operating the hiring platform.

Your Rights

  • Access: View your data via /candidates/data or /recruiters/data
  • Rectification: Update your profile via profile endpoints
  • Erasure: Delete your data via /candidates/data or /recruiters/data (DELETE)
  • Consent: You must provide explicit consent during registration; withdraw consent by deleting your account
  • Portability: Request data in a structured format (contact support)
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Data Security

We use encryption (e.g., BCrypt for passwords, JWT for authentication) and maintain audit logs to protect your data.

Data Retention

Data is retained until you request deletion or your account becomes inactive for 2 years, after which it is automatically deleted.

Contact Us

For questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@hiring-system.com.
